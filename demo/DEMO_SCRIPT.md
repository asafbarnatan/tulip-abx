## Beat 1: Mission Control (20 seconds)

[Open `localhost:3000/mission-control`]

> *"Every named account, every signal, every KPI, every agent — in one place. Seven SaaS-B2B KPIs computed live from the data: Pipeline Coverage, ICP Score, Signal Backlog, Play Activation Rate, Brief Approval, Account Velocity, and LinkedIn CTR. Seven-day sparklines on each tile show the trend."*

## Beat 2: Signal Watch agent (25 seconds)

[Click the **⚡ Run Signal Watch** button in the header]

> *"Watch — the SignalWatcherAgent is running right now. It's sweeping all five accounts, pulling unprocessed signals, updating intent scores where warranted, and ranking urgency with explicit reasoning. Claude Opus 4.6, live."*

[Live steps stream in the Agent Activity panel]

> *"Twelve seconds. And look at the reasoning — this isn't just pattern matching. That's a senior AE's judgment, expressed by an agent."*

## Beat 3: Per-account agentic workflow — Bayer (45 seconds)

[Click on **Bayer AG** in Account Pulse]

> *"Let me show you what happens when we go deep on one account."*

[Positioning Brief tab, already pre-generated]

> *"This is Bayer's positioning brief — generated by the PositioningAgent earlier today. Top of the page: an April Dunford-style positioning statement. Scannable in five seconds. Underneath, the Strategic Narrative — **internal AE briefing, never sent to the customer** — which maps the specific play: Wuppertal beachhead with Claudia Becker on electronic batch records, proving paperless operations under EMA scrutiny, then expanding EU-wide."*

> *"Three strategic pillars the AE anchors every conversation on. Per-persona messages for each buying-group contact — Dr. Müller gets the economic buyer framing, Claudia the champion framing, Marco the IT evaluator framing. Proof points and objection handlers underneath."*

[Click to **Agents** tab]

> *"And here's the full agent run history for this account. Every step, every tool call, every output — auditable."*

## Beat 4: LinkedIn integration — real campaign (25 seconds)

[Back to Mission Control → LinkedIn panel]

> *"You told me to integrate with a real LinkedIn campaign. I did. This campaign is live on LinkedIn right now — targeting manufacturing VPs, anchored in the agent-generated copy you just saw. **[IMPRESSIONS] impressions, [CLICKS] clicks in the first [DAYS] days**, all real data pulled from Campaign Manager."*

> *"The agent drafted the headline and ad copy. I reviewed it. I published to Campaign Manager. The human-in-the-loop matters — no enterprise lets an agent auto-publish ad spend without review."*

> *"This is what Agentic Marketing looks like operationally: autonomous agents doing strategic work, a Mission Control layer humans trust, real integrations where the stakes are real. Five agents, five named accounts, one real campaign, all in 6 days."*

## What NOT to do on stage

- ❌ Don't regenerate a brief live (takes 60-90s, will feel slow)
- ❌ Don't run a Full Pipeline on Bayer live (takes 4 min — too long)
- ❌ Don't click through all 6 account tabs (pick 2-3 at most)
- ❌ Don't show the Agents tab's raw JSON output (expand only one step, not all)
- ❌ Don't mention that Elena Richter was ever a fake AE name (fixed, forget it)
- ❌ Don't say "Claude" unless asked — frame as "the agent" / "the PositioningAgent"
- ❌ Don't promise features that aren't built (no Salesforce sync, no Email integration, no Pre-generated ROI model)
